WHO I AM

UI/UX Designer, graphic designer, photographer

Hi, I’m Nawela Ushan Dharmawardena, a multifaceted designer specializing in graphic design, UI/UX, and photography. My passion lies in crafting not just visuals but complete visual experiences that captivate and make a lasting impression.

Graphic Design
As a dedicated graphic designer, I am committed to transforming ideas into visual realities. With meticulous attention to detail and a profound understanding of design principles, I ensure every project resonates with its intended audience while enhancing your brand’s visual identity. From initial concept through to final execution, my goal is to deliver striking designs that perfectly align with your vision.
UI/UX Design
In the realm of UI/UX, my focus is on creating intuitive and engaging interfaces that enhance user interaction and satisfaction. Leveraging the latest tools and methodologies, I design seamless experiences for web and mobile applications. My approach involves a thorough understanding of user behavior and needs, ensuring that every design is not only beautiful but also functional and user-centric.
Photography
My photography extends beyond mere pictures; it captures stories. Whether it’s highlighting products or capturing the essence of a moment, my photographic work is about creating a narrative that speaks to the viewer. Combining technical skill with artistic flair, I strive to produce images that not only look stunning but also evoke emotions and connect on a deeper level.
